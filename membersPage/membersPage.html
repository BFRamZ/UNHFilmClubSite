Meet the Members

Matt Landry - President

About Me: I grew up in Berlin, New Hampshire and discovered my passion for filmmaking during my junior year of high school. In college, I went on to start the UNH Film Club with the goal in mind of building a group of creative minds who also love film. I was lucky to find an amazing group of people who I can all consider my friends. Oh yeah, my favorite movie is The Shining.
